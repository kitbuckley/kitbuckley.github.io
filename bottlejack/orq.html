open review quarterly

my friends Amelia, Adam, and Michael started the Open Review Quarterly, an online literary journal with each issues focused on a certain theme. over a span of three years, i contributed seven pieces, a few of which were illustrated by some amazing printmakers whose work i had admired. my favorites are probably the rhinestone macbeth (which really should have been called "plan nine from rhea county") and no escape starring ray liotta
